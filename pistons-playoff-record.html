The Chase Continues

From Worst to First: The Pistons' Historic Comeback

  • In 2023-24, the Detroit Pistons won just 14 games — the worst record in the NBA. The franchise appeared directionless, mired in a rebuild with no clear path forward.
  • In 2024-25, under head coach J.B. Bickerstaff, the Pistons won 44 games and returned to the playoffs for the first time since 2019, losing in the first round to the New York Knicks.
  • In 2025-26, the Pistons exploded to a 60-22 record — a 46-game improvement from their 14-win season just two years prior, earning the #1 seed in the Eastern Conference for the first time since 2007.
  • The turnaround was fueled by Cade Cunningham (the 2021 #1 overall pick), Tobias Harris (the veteran leader), Jalen Duren (the dominant center), and a deep supporting cast including Duncan Robinson and Daniss Jenkins.
  • In the 2026 First Round, the Pistons defeated the Orlando Magic 4-3 — the same team they last beat in a playoff series back in 2008.
  • Pistons legend Isiah Thomas praised the team's resurgence, noting how Cunningham and Harris embody the "Goin' to Work" ethos that defined Detroit's championship eras.

Franchise Playoff Timeline

  • 1988: Isiah Thomas scores 43 points on a sprained ankle in Game 6 of the NBA Finals vs Lakers — one of the most legendary performances in sports history. Pistons lose series in 7.
  • 1989: Pistons win first NBA Championship, sweeping the Lakers 4-0. Joe Dumars named Finals MVP.
  • 1990: Pistons repeat as champions, defeating Portland Trail Blazers 4-1. Isiah Thomas named Finals MVP.
  • 2004: The "Goin' to Work" Pistons — Chauncey Billups, Richard Hamilton, Tayshaun Prince, Ben Wallace, Rasheed Wallace — defeat the Lakers 4-1 for the franchise's third championship. Billups named Finals MVP.
  • 2005: Pistons return to NBA Finals, lose to San Antonio Spurs in 7 games.
  • 2008: Last playoff series win (before 2026 drought) — beat Orlando Magic 4-1 in Eastern Conference Semifinals. Last Conference Finals appearance.
  • 2008-2025: 17-year playoff series win drought. 11-game home playoff losing streak.
  • 2025: Pistons return to playoffs after 6-year absence, lose to Knicks in first round.
  • 2026: Win 60 games. Earn #1 seed. Win first playoff series in 18 years. End 11-game home playoff losing streak. Currently in Eastern Conference Semifinals.
